Task 6: Explanation for the misscalssification

The models mistake because:
1. Looks alike: “Good” and “Moderate” can look almost the same. A slightly hazy or cloudy clean day may trick the model into saying “Moderate.”
2. Lighting and time: Sunrise, sunset, or strong shadows change sky colors. A dark evening sky can look like heavy pollution.
3. Blocked sky: If buildings, trees, or other objects hide most of the sky, the model has too little sky to check.
4. Camera quality: Blurry photos, sun glare, or low-resolution images add noise that the model can confuse with smog or dust.
